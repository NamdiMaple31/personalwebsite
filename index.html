Who The Heck is Namdi???

What's up my name is Namdi Nesta Maple. Born on September 12, 2002, I am a fifteen year old African-American male with big plans. I live with my mother, my father, and my two siblings. My grandparents on my mothers side play a big part in my life to the point that they are almost like a second set of parents. My grandparents on my fathers side are around too, but they live far and I don't see them as often. My family owns a children's education center that was founded by my grandparents back in the year 1992. My family is well known throughout my community for their hardwork and business endeavors. Seriously I can't go anywhere with them without them seeing someone they know. They have quite the history! Needless to say my family has done an excellent job paving the way for my siblings and I and I firmly believe that what ever happens in the future
